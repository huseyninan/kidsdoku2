# KidsDoku App Screens

This document provides an overview of the screens in the KidsDoku app, detailing their purpose and features.

## 1. Main Menu
The starting point of the adventure, featuring a friendly fox background.

*   **Visuals**: A warm, inviting "Quest Log" style interface.
*   **Game Modes**:
    *   **3x3 First Steps**: Perfect for the youngest players to learn the basics.
    *   **4x4 Easy Journey**: For beginners to start their adventure.
    *   **6x6 Brave Expedition**: A slightly larger challenge for brave explorers.
*   **Features**:
    *   **How to Play**: Quick access to the tutorial to learn the rules.
    *   **Settings**: Access to app configuration.
    *   **Go Premium**: Option to unlock full features (if applicable).

## 3. Game Screen
The main puzzle-solving interface, designed like a storybook.

*   **Visuals**: A colorful board set against a backdrop of clouds and hills.
*   **Gameplay**:
    *   **The Board**: A 3x3, 4x4, or 6x6 grid to fill with symbols.
    *   **Veggie Basket**: The palette of symbols (animals/veggies) to place on the board.
*   **Tools**:
    *   **Undo**: Step back if you make a mistake.
    *   **Erase**: Remove a symbol from a square.
    *   **Hint**: Get help if you're stuck (shows a lightbulb).
*   **Status**:
    *   **Timer**: Tracks how long you've been playing.
    *   **Progress Bar**: Shows how close you are to finishing.
*   **Celebration**: Confetti and a "You Did It!" card appear when a puzzle is solved, showing your star rating.

## 4. How to Play (Tutorial)
A step-by-step guide to learning Sudoku.

*   **Steps**:
    1.  **Introduction**: Explains the goal of filling empty boxes.
    2.  **Rows**: Teaches that each symbol appears once per row.
    3.  **Columns**: Teaches that each symbol appears once per column.
    4.  **Boxes**: Teaches that each symbol appears once per small box area.
*   **Interactive**: Visual examples showing correct and incorrect placements.

## 5. Settings
Manage app preferences and data.

*   **Audio & Feedback**: Toggle sound effects and vibration (haptics).
*   **Progress Management**: Options to reset progress for specific puzzle sizes or the entire game.
*   **Information**: View the "About" section, Privacy Policy, and app version.
